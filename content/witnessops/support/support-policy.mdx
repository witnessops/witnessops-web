# Support Policy

WitnessOps support runs through the verified support mailbox and the public support form. A request only enters the queue after mailbox verification, so the queue reflects a confirmed sender rather than an unverified inbound address.

## Support channels

- Public support form: [/support](/support)
- Verified support mailbox: [support@witnessops.com](mailto:support@witnessops.com)

Use support for product questions, verification help, access issues, and policy-gate questions that are not security incidents.

## Response target

General support aims for a first response within 1 business day. This is the current public target, not a 24/7 guarantee.

## Security issues

Report vulnerabilities through responsible disclosure at [security@witnessops.com](mailto:security@witnessops.com). The broader disclosure policy lives at [Security](/security). Security reports do not go through the normal support queue.

## Limits

WitnessOps does not promise 24/7 live support, instant resolution, or emergency incident response through the support mailbox.
