TEMP CONTACT/SUPPORT FORM CHANGE (2026-04-23)

What changed
- /review/request POSTs to /api/contact for direct email follow-up, no mailbox verification.
- /api/contact sends a plain-text review request email to engage@witnessops.com via the configured mail provider.
- The support page form POSTs to /api/support/message for direct email follow-up, no admin queue write.
- /api/support/message sends a plain-text support request email to support@witnessops.com via the configured mail provider.
- Inventory + api-contract distinguish the email-only public form paths from retained queue-backed verified APIs.
- Mailbox config uses WITNESSOPS_MAILBOX_ENGAGE and WITNESSOPS_MAILBOX_SUPPORT.
